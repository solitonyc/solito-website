Biography

Meeting Bill Gates

In 1998, working as a Tech Specialist for the Sullivan & Cromwell law firm, a lucky opportunity came my way.

My manager assigned me to work with the legal team stationed in Washington DC. I was solely responsible for providing all around IT and network support for the legal team, who were now fully engaged with defending Bill Gates, and his company Microsoft against the United States of America in the famed “Antitrust Trial of the Century.”

It was an honor to meet Bill Gates himself and other Microsoft executives, such as Steve Ballmer, Jim Allchin, Paul Moritz & Bill Neukom.

Leading the team were highly regarded corporate attornies John Warden and Steve Holly, high-profile partners of the firm who were constantly appearing in newspapers and on TV in regards to the trial.

I found great honor having the opportunity to meet Bill Gates himself and other Microsoft executives such as Steve Ballmer, Jim Allchin, Paul Moritz & Bill Neukom. I greatly appreciated this extraordinary experience considering Bill Gates, my hero, had inspired me to go back to school in order to get in the tech industry.

This fortuitous opportunity catapult me through my exciting tech future ahead.

Installing Sattelite Systems Across America

I had already moved on before the crash, by taking on the role of Senior IT Executive at RAW Communications. I traveled frequently across the US, managing a team of satellite engineers implementing dishes and receivers on the rooftops of the biggest buildings for many of the most prestigious financial institutions in the world, including the original World Trade Center and Fidelity tower in Boston. Our clients were well known, such as Oppenheimer, Brandeis, AIM, State Street and Fidelity. RAW provided the means for traders, portfolio and wealth managers to view live, corporate officers presenting their company's quarterly financial reports directly on their screens (pre-broadband era). RAW was eventually bought out by ThomsonReuters.
